About Me







Hi there and welcome,
I started building this page to showcase projects I would like to share - and also to learn some html ;) . I am trying to explain my projects and steps I take in detail as I know how confusing it is to look at some (random) code snippets without description.

It has been quite a journey for me transitioning from a supply chain/ business intelligence background to data science and analytics, so I hope this may be helpful for other aspiring data scientists and data analysts as well. I personally work at the intersection of business, data and technology. During my business analytics program I gained stats and coding skills that I enhanced with my business knowledge to solve complex business problems. Understanding the business (problem) is oftentimes very challenging but crucial in order to start the analysis part. Only when having a clear goal in mind, analytics can be turned into insights and impact strategy as well as operations.

Currently, I work as data and analytics consultant in the bay area. After studying at UC Davis I always wanted to go come back. I enjoy working out and swimming during my downtime. Now, during these weird times, I spend a lot of time outdoors exploring city and nature. Cheers, Phil
